Let's get to know your enemy

To begin, as with any difficult boss, you must know what they are capable of. This guy is capable of a lot. Let's begin with the basics of this fight. First the boss himself and the arena:

  • The Fume Knight has 10,100 health in a new game and 14,140 health in NG+ after that his health will increase about 25%-50% each playthrough
  • He has two forms:
    1. His first form. He wields one quick hitting Fume Sword, and his much larger, and slower Fume Ultra Greatsword.
    2. His 0% fun mode. At around 50%-60% health he will drop his small sword and light the Ultra Greatsword on fire. Dark magic fire.
  • He can deal a very minuscule amount of fire damage when his sword glows in his first form, and he deals a huge chunk of both dark and fire damage in his second form.
  • As a DLC boss, he takes the regular 15% general elemental damage reduction for bosses and another still unknown damage reduction against attacks dealing only one type of elemental damage
  • His elemental resistance goes from most to least defence as follows: Fire > Lightning > Magic/Dark
  • He immune to the red eye rings effects.
  • There are four Ashen Idols outside his boss room, they will heal him making the battle even more difficult unless you destroy them.
  • You can summon two NPC's for this fight, however neither is worth their tiny health and minuscule damage consdiering the defence and health he gains from each being present
  • Lastly if you hear Velstadt's helm (his brothers helm) into battle he will go into his second form first thing.

To properly prepare for this fight you must be prepared for his second form, and that his moveset actually completely changes, meaning you must memorize two of them. Great.
